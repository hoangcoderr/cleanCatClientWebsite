Frequently Asked Questions

Is CleanCatClient compatible with other mods?

Yes, CleanCatClient is designed to be compatible with most popular Minecraft mods. However, some mods may conflict with certain features. Check our compatibility list for details.

Will CleanCatClient work with multiplayer servers?

CleanCatClient is designed to be compatible with most vanilla and modded servers. However, some servers may have anti-cheat systems that could flag certain features. We recommend checking server rules before using all features.

Is CleanCatClient safe to use?

Absolutely! CleanCatClient is malware-free and safe to use. Our code is regularly audited for security vulnerabilities, and we take user privacy very seriously.

How do I update to a newer version?

Simply download the latest version and run the installer. It will automatically update your existing installation while preserving your settings.

I'm having technical issues. Where can I get help?

Visit our Support Forum or join our Discord server for technical assistance. You can also create a support ticket from your profile page.
