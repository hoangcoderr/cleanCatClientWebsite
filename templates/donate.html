Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

No, donations to CleanCatClient are not tax-deductible as we are not a registered non-profit organization.

How quickly will I receive my donor perks?

Most perks are automatically applied within 24 hours. For special items like custom capes, it may take up to 3 business days.

Can I donate with cryptocurrencies?

Yes, we accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, and several other cryptocurrencies. Select the "Crypto" option at checkout.

I donated but haven't received my perks. What should I do?

Please contact our support team through the support tab in your profile, and we'll resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Can I upgrade my donation tier later?

Yes, you can make additional donations at any time to reach higher tier benefits.
